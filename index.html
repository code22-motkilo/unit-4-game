Crystal Collector

You wil be given a random number at the start of each game.


There are 4 buttons below. When you click a button you will add a specific amount of points to your score.


You win a game by matching your total button score to the random value, you lose the game if your score exceeds the random number.


The value of each button is hidden, until you click on it and it is added to your score.


Each time a new game starts, the values of each but resets.
